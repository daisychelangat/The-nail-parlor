Description and Mission Statement

Description

Established in the year 2015, The Nail Doctor Parlour & Spa is a popular salon in Downtown Phoenix. From haircut, styling to color and other hair treatments, and spa services, the salon offers numerous services for its clients.

Hair Salon & Spa Mission Statement

We celebrate women and men in their real, most raw, authentic brilliance, who believe in themselves and are ready to step into the look they love. We employ hair products with some of the most natural and rich ingredients because we believe you should never expect less.

Services we offer

  • Manicure
  • Pedicure
  • Acrylic overlays and extensions
  • Dip powder nails
  • SNS nails
  • Nail design
  • Fibreglass overlays and extensions
